About NAIP

Background:
We are a group of Nigerian Americans leveraging our professional background and years of experience across multiple industries to empower founders and entrepreneurs through investments, advisory and support.

Vision:
Providing access to knowledge and capital for entrepreneurs seeking to nurture an idea and grow it to fruition.

Philosophy:
Build generational wealth by investing in businesses that not only seek profits but also leave a positive impact in their communities.
