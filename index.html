Our Story

We are friends. We are Nigerian who live in the Dallas metro area. During a virtual happy hour at the onset of the COVID pandemic, we shared our frustrations with the crisis and the economic recession that was looming. We were livid about how much we had lost in the stock market and in other investment portfolios. We also knew that some people were in better positions to ride the waves of such economic and pandemic crisis to their financial gain. Realizing the need to make some changes in our respective financial strategies, we set out on a mission to pull our resources together and seek out investment opportunities that we may not typically have access to individually. As immigrants, our journey started from the bottom. But now we are determined to secure the future of the next generation by creating generational wealth through strategic investments in businesses and ventures.

Our background spans more than 100 years of combined experience across multiple sectors, domains, disciplines and competencies including but not limited to medicine/care delivery, legal, corporate and business strategy, technology enablement, financial operations, complex program management and risk management. We leverage our relationship, sphere of influence and collective financial strength to make an impact on our mission.

We are community focused. We are not a charity organization, but we support up and coming Nigerian entrepreneurs and African founders in the Diaspora. We mentor, advise and guide because we understand that sometimes our experience is a more valuable investment than money.

About NAIP

Our Vision

  • To be the pre-eminent group of professionals focused on growing the wealth and influence of Nigerians in the diaspora.


Our Mission

  • Create access for NAIP members by leveraging our collective relationships, sphere of influence and our financial and economic capital in order to achieve the NAIP vision

  • Empower Nigerian founders and entrepreneurs through investment, strategic support and advisory

  • Create, grow and sustain a platform to access private market investment opportunities
